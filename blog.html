COOK STOVE MAKING

The Canarumwe (local vernacular, Kinyarwanda, meaning “use one firewood”) is a simple clay cook stove that can reduce firewood use by more than 50% compared to the traditional open fires and is easy and convenient to use, helping households to take that first step away from open fires.
