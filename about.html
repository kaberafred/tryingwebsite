Action for Sustainable Transformation

  • About
  • Mission
  • Vision
Action for Sustainable Transformation is a Local NGO with operations in Rwanda and aimed at transforming vulnerable communities through a holistic approach focusing mainly on Providing quality education to students, Economic empowerment for mainly youth and women and Health programs working hand in hand with the communities on preventive measure to health challenges, forinstance Community health workers program,Nutrition and hygiene and Sanitation
To promote community Empowerment and wellbeing through quality Education, Lifeskills development and community based health, nutrition, hygiene and sanittion services
AST believes that to achieve SDGs by 2030, there is need for concerted efforts and collaboration by different practitioners and working closely with the communities (beneficiaries for ownership) as key stakeholders, skills development through education at different levels and availing easy access to finance and financial literacy to the masses coupled with promoting the saving culture and lastly but not least, comeup with preventive measures for health challenges to reduce on huge expenditure on treatment
